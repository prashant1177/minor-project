Mental Health and Well-Being Surveillance


At Mental Health Surveillance, we are dedicated to providing individuals with tools to assess their mental well-being and to promote mental health awareness. Our platform offers a simple yet effective survey designed to gauge various aspects of mental health.

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their mental health by providing them with valuable insights and resources. Through our survey, users can gain a better understanding of their mental well-being and take proactive steps towards self-care and seeking professional help if needed.

We understand the importance of mental health and the impact it has on overall well-being. That's why we strive to make our platform accessible, user-friendly, and confidential. Your privacy is our priority, and all survey responses are kept confidential and anonymous.

Join us in our mission to promote mental health awareness and support individuals in their journey towards better mental well-being. Together, we can break the stigma surrounding mental health and create a more supportive and understanding community.
